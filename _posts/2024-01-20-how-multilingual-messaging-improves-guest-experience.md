## Why Language Matters in Guest Communication

Guests often travel across borders and book properties in countries where they do not speak the local language. Misunderstandings can easily occur when:

- check‑in instructions are unclear  
- house rules are not fully understood  
- emergency information is not translated  
- platform messages appear inconsistent  

Providing multilingual communication solves these problems and builds trust from the very first interaction.
